But First... About Me

My name is Josh and I am a bartender! For the last few years, I have been tinkering, mixing, shaking and tasting from Manchester to Melbourne, honing my skills and learning from other excellent industry friends. From creating new and exciting cocktails with home-made shrubs and foams, to reviving old, lost and forgotten tinctures from centuries past - it is a job I thoroughly enjoy.
