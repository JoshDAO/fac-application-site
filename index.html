About Me

My name is Josh and I am a bartender! For the last few years, I have been tinkering, mixing, shaking and tasting from Manchester to Melbourne, honing my skills and learning from other excellent industry friends. From creating new and exciting cocktails with home-made shrubs and foams, to reviving old, lost and forgotten tinctures from centuries past - it is an industry I have thoroughly enjoyed being a part of. Over there in my state of the art *cough* image carousel, are a few shots of me doing my thing. I never planned this career path, it was rather out of necessity; I wanted to be an astronaut throughout my childhood (okay I still do a little). However, I found the theory and skills to be interesting, and eventually took up a training role within a bar, where I could share my knowledge and passion.
